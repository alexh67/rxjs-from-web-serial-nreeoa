Serial Chat Example

Welcome to the RxJS Ninja Serial Chat example. This operator works with the new Web Serial API, allowing communication between the browser and serial devices

To get started the easiest way is to use Bluetooth Serial with a mobile device:

  • For Android - Serial Bluetooth Terminal
  • For iOS - BLE to Serial Terminal
You can use Ctrl + C to connect and Ctrl + D to disconnect.

Enter text in the area below and press "Enter" or click the button.

You can click the Emoji buttons below to add them to the text box, you can also press Ctrl + 0 - 9 on your keyboard to enter the first few Emoji using shortcuts.
